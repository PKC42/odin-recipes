Hand Jam

This is one pretty nasty. The OP of this post shows an image of a plate of cream and a hand shaped patch of jam.

This is pretty simple. All you need is:

  • Cream
  • Jam
  • Your Hand
  • A plate
  • A torch

Simple do the following

  1. Pipe the cream onto the plate
  2. Torch the cream until light brown
  3. Press your hand into some jam
  4. Press your hand into the plate

And you are done. To see the original post, visit here.
